How to make the best burger in the world

Description

Burgers are the best. Mushed cow bits, packed together, cooked then a slice of curdled cow juice added to the top, what's not to love?!

Seriously, they're deeeeeeelicious. Mmmm, mmmm, mmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmmm

Ingredients

  • 1 burger
  • 1 slice of buffalo tomato
  • 1 slice of easi-single cheese
  • 1 leaf of lettuce
  • red sauce
  • mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Grill the burger
  2. Toast the bun
  3. Squirt red sauce on the bottom bun
  4. Put slice of tomato on top of sauce
  5. Put burger on top of tomato
  6. Put cheese on top of burger
  7. Put lettuce on top of cheese
  8. Put mayonnaise on top of lettuce
  9. Put top bun on top of mayonnaise
  10. eat
