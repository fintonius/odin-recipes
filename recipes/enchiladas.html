Tasty Enchiladas!

Description

One of the main reasons why Green Enchiladas are the favourite dish for most Mexicans is not only from its ancestral origins, which are brought back with the flavor of essential ingredients used in Mexican cuisine back in the days, but also due to its easy preparation and variety of fillings and sides dishes it can be serve with.

Who has not yet tried some delicious Green Enchiladas cannot call himself Mexican or say that they have visited our culture, since aside from tacos, pozole, antojitos, etc, enchiladas are a must.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups Shredded chicken
  • 1/2 cup Chopped white onion
  • 1/4 cup Chopped cilantro
  • 1 pack Corn Tortillas 15 cm
  • 2 1/2 cups Salsa de Tomatillo Verde
  • 1/2 cup Mexican Style Queso fresco

For the Salsa de Tomatillo Verde (from scracth)

  • 1 can – Whole Green Tomatillo
  • 1 Jalapeno (fresh)
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • 1 tbs – Corn or vegetable oil
  • 1/3 cup – Chicken broth
  • Salt & Pepper
